Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

The IMDB movies dataset contains a list of movies, unique imdb id and other properties.The different columns in the dataset contains properties of the movie.IMDB(Internet Movie Database) is a web site that provides information about millions of films and television programs as well as their cast and directors. Below is the list of columns contained in the dataset and their meaning.

  • id- this is the unique number for a specific movie
  • imdb_id- this is imdb's unique number for each movie
  • popularity- The popularity rating of a particular title on IMDb indicates how much that title's page has been visited
  • budget- this is the amount of money spent to produce the movie
  • Revenue- this is amount made from sales of the movie
  • original_title- this is the title of the movie
  • Cast- these are the names of the actors and actresses in the movie
  • Homepage- this is website of the movie
  • Director- the name(s) of the directors of the movie
  • tagline- this is a short text which serves to clarify the movie
  • keywords- this is used to describe any notable object, concept, style or action that takes place during the movie
  • overview- this is used to summarize the film's storyline
  • runtime- this is time duration of the film in minutes
  • genres- this is categories that define a movie based on its narrative elements.
  • production_companies- this contains a list of companies that produced the movies
  • release_date- this is the date the film was released
  • vote_count- IMDb registered users can cast a vote (from 1 to 10) on every released title in the database
  • vote_average- this is the average of the vote count
  • release_year- this is the year the film was released
  • budget_adj- this is budget of the associated movie in terms of 2010 dollars, accounting for inflation over time
  • revenue_adj- this is the revenue of the associated movie in terms of 2010 dollars, accounting for inflation over time.
